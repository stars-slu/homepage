Welcome to STARS@SLU

Curious about data? Want to try research? No experience needed!

STARS@SLU is a free two-week summer program for high school juniors and seniors in the St. Louis area. You will learn to work with real data using R, a programming language, while exploring datasets on topics you care about, from St. Louis Open Data to national databases. Working in small teams with support from SLU faculty and student mentors, you will investigate questions that interest you, analyze what you find, and present your results at the end of the program during the poster session. Throughout the program, you will solve problems through math games and puzzles, hear from professionals in different fields, and connect with other students who are curious about STEM.

What STARS@SLU Offers

  • Scholarship support for all participants
  • Daily breakfast and lunch provided
  • Transportation support
  • Computing resources and software (personal laptops welcome but not required)
  • Mentorship from SLU faculty and student mentors
  • Program t-shirt and certificate of completion
