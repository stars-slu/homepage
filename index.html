About STARS@SLU

STARS@SLU (Summer Training and Research Scholars at Saint Louis University) is a program designed to provide undergraduates with hands-on research experience in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields.

Participants work alongside faculty mentors on original research projects, attend seminars, and present their results at the end of the program. STARS@SLU aims to foster curiosity, innovation, and academic excellence.
